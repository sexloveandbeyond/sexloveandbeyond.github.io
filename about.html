About

I am Dr. Rhonda Lewis, researching and lecturing psychology at the University of Michigan.
My research interests center on sex, love and relationships. With this blog, I want to give a little bit of an inside in my work. I will be publishing articles that appear to have relevance for the broad public. Beyond that I will also include posts merely consisting of personal thoughts to encourage discussion.
Have fun reading!
